Skeletron

Biography

Dark, sharp and energetic — the visual identity mirrors a sound built for impact.

Skeletron is a Goa-based DJ, music producer and educator whose work lives between performance energy and studio precision. His artist world draws from club culture, Bollywood remix momentum, electronic textures and a high-contrast visual language.

As a performer, Skeletron is built for rooms that need movement — from intimate showcases to high-energy events. His sets are designed with narrative pacing, punchy transitions and a strong understanding of what makes a crowd react.

As a producer, his identity is rooted in remix culture and modern electronic arrangement. Press coverage around his Bollywood remix work has helped position him as a Goan artist on the right track, combining local origin with a larger contemporary sound.

Through Skeletron Music Academy, he brings that same process to students. The academy focuses on DJ fundamentals, music production, mixing, mastering, creative direction and artist confidence — not just software lessons, but the mindset behind finishing music.

The mission is simple: create music that lands hard, looks premium, and gives upcoming artists a practical path into the world of DJing and production.

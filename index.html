Who Is
Skeletron

Skeletron is a DJ, producer, performer and music mentor shaping a bold electronic sound from Goa, India. His world blends Bollywood remix energy, club-ready production, and a polished visual identity made for modern stages.

From acclaimed remixes to academy-led training, Skeletron connects performance and education — helping listeners feel the drop and helping creators understand how it was built.

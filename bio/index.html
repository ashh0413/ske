BIOGRAPHY

Skeletron is a Goa based DJ, music producer, and educator whose work lives between performance energy and studio precision. His artist world blends club culture, Bollywood remix momentum, electronic textures, and a bold, high contrast visual identity.

Beyond electronic music, Skeletron has played a key role in bringing Konkani music to a wider audience. His hit singles "Nach Re", with over 2 million YouTube views, and "Ringa Ringa", with over 1 million YouTube views, have helped push modern Konkani music into the mainstream, reaching audiences not only across Goa but also within Konkani communities in London, the USA, and Dubai.

As a performer, Skeletron is built for rooms that need movement from intimate showcases to large scale festivals and high energy events. His sets are driven by narrative pacing, seamless transitions, tribal rhythms, and an instinctive understanding of what makes a crowd react.

As a producer, his identity is rooted in remix culture and modern electronic production. His Bollywood remixes and original releases have earned industry recognition, helping position him as one of Goa's most exciting electronic artists while connecting local influences with a contemporary global sound.

Through Skeletron Music Academy, he shares that same creative process with the next generation of artists. The academy teaches DJ fundamentals, music production, mixing, mastering, creative direction, and artist development going beyond software tutorials to build the mindset and discipline required to create professional music and perform with confidence.

The mission is simple: create music that hits hard, build a premium creative brand, and give aspiring artists a practical, real world pathway into DJing, music production, and the modern music industry.

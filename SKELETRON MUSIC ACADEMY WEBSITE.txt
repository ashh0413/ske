SKELETRON MUSIC ACADEMY WEBSITE 

https://www.skeletronmusic.com/home

I’ve gone through the website.

### Overall Rating

**Design:** 8.5/10
**User Experience:** 7.5/10
**Performance:** 7/10
**Conversion:** 6.5/10

The website is visually appealing and clearly positions Skeletron as a DJ, producer, and educator, showcasing music, performances, courses, and contact information. It highlights major achievements, embedded videos, and social links, but it could do a better job guiding visitors toward specific actions such as booking shows or enrolling in courses. ([Skeletron Music][1])

## What's Good

* Strong branding with a dark theme that matches the music industry.
* Professional hero section introducing Skeletron.
* Social media links are immediately visible.
* Embedded music and videos keep visitors engaged.
* Academy section is detailed and builds credibility.
* Simple navigation (Home, Academy, Music, Tour, Contact). ([Skeletron Music][1])

## What Can Be Improved

### 1. Hero Section

The landing section feels a bit static.

Instead:

* Add a cinematic full-screen background video.
* Add one clear CTA:

  * Book Now
  * Join Academy
  * Listen Now

### 2. Typography

There is too much plain text in the biography.

Instead:

* Break it into smaller sections.
* Use larger headings.
* Highlight achievements with numbers.

Example:

* 100+ Shows
* Millions of Streams
* Sony Music Artist
* Shared Stage with Martin Garrix

### 3. Animations

The page feels static.

Add:

* Scroll animations
* Text reveals
* Hover effects
* Smooth transitions
* Interactive gallery

### 4. Academy Page

The course content is comprehensive but overwhelming because it appears as one long page. ([Skeletron Music][2])

Improve by:

* Course cards
* Expand/collapse modules
* Comparison table
* "Enroll Now" buttons
* Student testimonials with photos

### 5. Tour Page

The Tour page only says "Coming Soon." ([Skeletron Music][3])

Instead include:

* Upcoming events
* Previous performances
* Interactive map
* Ticket links
* Photo gallery

### 6. Music Section

The music page could better showcase releases.

Consider:

* Album cards
* Spotify embeds
* Streaming statistics
* Featured releases
* Playlist previews

### 7. Contact

The contact page is functional but basic. ([Skeletron Music][4])

Add:

* WhatsApp button
* Booking calendar
* Management details
* FAQ
* Response time
* Google Maps location (if relevant)

### 8. Social Proof

The homepage mentions major artists, but visual proof would make a stronger impression. ([Skeletron Music][1])

Examples:

* Logos of Sony Music, Zee Music, Sunburn
* Press mentions
* Awards
* Fan testimonials

### 9. Mobile Experience

The site likely works on mobile, but it could feel more premium with:

* Larger buttons
* Sticky navigation
* Faster image loading
* Better spacing

### 10. Premium Feel

To make it feel like a modern, high-end artist website:

* Glassmorphism
* Custom cursor
* Animated backgrounds
* Parallax scrolling
* Loading animation
* Smooth page transitions
* High-quality micro-interactions

## Overall

The website already has solid content and a clear brand identity, but it feels closer to a polished template than a premium custom experience. With modern motion design, stronger calls to action, better content hierarchy, and richer visual storytelling, it could reach the level of websites built for internationally recognized artists.

A redesign focused on animations, storytelling, and conversion could realistically improve it from **8.5/10 to around 9.8/10** while making it feel significantly more memorable and engaging.

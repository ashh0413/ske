Security

Last updated: July 2026

Skeletron Music takes the security of your information seriously. This page outlines our security practices.

Data Protection

  • All data transmitted through our website uses HTTPS encryption
  • Personal information is stored securely and access is restricted
  • Regular security reviews are conducted on our systems
  • Third-party services we use are vetted for security compliance

Payment Security

  • Payment processing is handled by trusted third-party providers
  • We do not store credit card or banking details on our servers
  • All payment transactions are encrypted
  • Payment providers are PCI-DSS compliant

Website Security

  • Our website is protected against common web vulnerabilities
  • Regular backups are performed to prevent data loss
  • Access to our content management systems is restricted and monitored
  • Security patches are applied promptly

What You Can Do

  • Use strong, unique passwords for any accounts
  • Be cautious of phishing attempts — we will never ask for passwords via email
  • Keep your contact information up to date
  • Report any suspicious activity to us immediately

Incident Response

In the event of a security incident, we will notify affected users within 72 hours and work to resolve the issue promptly.

Contact

If you have security concerns or have identified a vulnerability, contact us at skeletronmusic7@gmail.com.
